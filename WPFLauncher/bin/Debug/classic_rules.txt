You have chosen to play on one of the servers that Mythic Entertainment has specifically designated as a Non-Trials of Atlantis and Ranged Concentration Buff Server.  This server differs from the standard DAoC servers in the following ways:


 - Players cannot access the lands of Atlantis on this server.  All abilities and items that require the Trials of Atlantis Expansion are not available.

 - All concentration buffs on this server are set at a range of 5000 maximum units, and are available only to the caster and members of the casters group.

 - There is no /level 20 available on this server.


All rules from the DAoC Rules of Conduct apply to this server.  Welcome to the Classic Server, and have fun playing Dark Age of Camelot!
